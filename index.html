Crimes in Cities in Washington State Project

This is an interactive map that records crimes happening in Washington State in real time. Any user of this interactive map can record crime incidents or observe the crime situation around them through the map. The target audience for this map is the residents of the state, and its purpose is to provide the latest crime situations to the residents of Washington State to protect their safety and further reduce the state's crime rate. You can fill in the crime situations of nearby areas in real time, so all users can see the crime situation in each area and plan their next moves and self-protection.
